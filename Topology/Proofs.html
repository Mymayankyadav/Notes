8.9 Theorem

If \(\tau\) is any topology on the product space in which \(\pi_{\beta})'s are continuous then for each \(\beta\), if \(U_{\beta}\) is open in \(X_{\beta}\) then its inverse image under \(\pi\) is open in \(\tau\). Consequently, the member of subasis of Tychonoff topology is open in \(\tau\) and Tychonoff topology is contained \(\tau\).
